SyncNote Alpha Dev Release

2022, Aug 25

I am happy to announce the alpha release of SyncNote, a simple notes app for Android and PC which lets you easily synchronize your notes between your mobile and PC devices. The app is developed using Jetpack Compose and Jetbrains Compose Desktop for the JVM release, I used Kotlin Multiplatform to share source-code between platforms. I developed this app during the last few months as a personal exercise.
